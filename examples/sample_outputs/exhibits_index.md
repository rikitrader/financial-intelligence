## Exhibit Foundation Requirements

### Business Records (FRE 803(6))

Foundation elements:
1. Record made at or near the time
2. By person with knowledge
3. Kept in course of regularly conducted activity
4. Regular practice to make such records
5. Shown by custodian testimony or certification

### Expert Materials (FRE 703)

Foundation elements:
1. Expert qualifications established
2. Methodology is reliable
3. Methodology properly applied
4. Based on sufficient facts/data
